Quesadillas

Description

Quesadillas are a tasty combination of tortillas, cheese and various toppings. This recipe can be fine tuned as ypu wish.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Cheese
  • Bean Chili Salsa
  • Toppings

Steps to prepare

  1. Chop Toppings
  2. Mix toppings and salsa
  3. Pan fry everything
