Market Salad

Description

Go down to your closest grocery store and fill up a bucket on the expensive deli salad bar!

As with he other recipes, this one also has multiple ways of tuning so you won't ever get bored

It gets a bit expensive so try to take as little as possible...

Ingredients

  • Paper bowl
  • Plastic cover
  • Salad
  • Anything else you want
  • A payment possibility

Steps to prepare

  1. Walk to the salad bar
  2. Grab a bowl
  3. Start fillin' it up
  4. Put the finished bowl onto the scale and get he bar code sticker
  5. Don't forget to pay
